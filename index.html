About us

Wild Fenn (previously known as Engel and the Mountain People) are an Indie/Folk band based in Devon, UK. Songwriters Luke Engel and Jess Illingworth love writing songs with beautiful harmonies that work both in live performances and in recordings. Although sometimes performing as a duo, Wild Fenn generally perform live as a 4-piece.

Wild Fenn’s latest single Wild Hearts was played by BBC Introducing on BBC Radio Cornwall and BBC Radio Devon.

“Rising and falling harmonies combined with poetic lyrics paint pictures of summer nights and bygone days. Wild Fenn’s songs are heavily inspired by the natural world and their lyrics aim to convey a subtle urgency to remember, cherish and protect our wild places.” - BBC Introducing

Wild Hearts

Wild Hearts is about adventure, freedom, and a connection with nature. The song leads you down a wild road, taking you away from the city, through an old pine gate into the forest. It’s a song about the idea of running with wild hearts and making your name known in the world, despite the challenges you encounter. This song is Wild Fenn’s first release with our newest member, Jess Illingworth, on guitar and vocals.

Check out the live session of our latest single Wild Hearts on YouTube. We filmed this in Plymbridge Woods (surrounded by lots of wild garlic!). Thank you to Louis Goldman for the videography.

Luke

Luke Engel (main vocals, harmonies, acoustic guitar, electric guitar, mandolin) - Luke loves being outdoors and you can often find him wandering in wild places looking for cool mushrooms and inspiration for his songwriting.

Jess

Jess Illingworth (main vocals, harmonies, acoustic guitar, mandolin) - Jess is known by the rest of the band as ‘one take Jess’. She loves walking by the coast and writing lyrics that reach out to people.

Matt

Matthew Gordon Price (percussion) - Matt, who almost exclusively wears black and a pair of very round sunglasses, loves any excuse to get out the djembe drum.

Olly

Olly Parker (bass, harmonies) - Olly is the most laid back member of Wild Fenn and enjoys sneaking funky bass lines into the songs.
